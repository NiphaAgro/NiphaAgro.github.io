About us


Nipha Agro is one of the upcoming leading integrated exporters of fresh produce from India. Fresh grapes, vegetables, and dehydrated food items are among the products Nipha Agro produces for export from India.  Located in Nashik, the Wine Capital and Grape Capital of India, we offer a wide variety of grapes and wine.

Nashik has located about 180 KM, North East of Mumbai. Having a world-class infrastructure allows us to deliver quality products at a consistent and sustainable pace.

We’re a group of farmers who have built a business to change the world.

Nipha Agro is creating new product categories for export from India including fresh grapes , vegetables and dehydrated food products. With a team of experienced professionals and a passion for excellence, we are committed to delivering the best to our customers. From sourcing to shipping, we manage every aspect of the supply chain to ensure the freshest, most flavorful produce arrives at your door. We work closely with local farmers to promote sustainable agriculture practices and support the local community.

At Nipha Agro, we believe that access to fresh, nutritious food should be a right for everyone. That's why we strive to make it easy for customers around the world to enjoy the unique flavors and health benefits of Indian agriculture. Nipha Agro is your trusted source for the finest produce from India.

Join us in our mission to nourish the world, one harvest at a time.
